Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Klippod, we receive basic profile information (such as your display name and TikTok user ID) and an access token that lets the Service post content on your behalf, as authorized by you through TikTok's official login flow.

2. How We Use It

We use this information solely to publish clips to your TikTok account at your direction and to manage your connection to the Service. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and account information are stored securely and used only to operate the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Revoking Access

You can disconnect your TikTok account from Klippod at any time, either within the Service or through your TikTok account's app permissions settings. Once disconnected, we no longer have posting access to your account.

5. Third-Party Services

Klippod uses TikTok's official APIs to operate. Your use of TikTok remains subject to TikTok's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
