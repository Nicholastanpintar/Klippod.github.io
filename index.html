Klippod

Automated short-form clip posting for content creators

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

Klippod helps content creators repurpose long-form video into short clips and publish them to TikTok on their behalf, with each creator's explicit authorization.

Creators connect their TikTok account through TikTok's official login and posting permissions. Klippod never posts without a creator's consent, and each creator can disconnect their account at any time.
